Welcome to the Rock, Paper, Scissors multiplayer game!!!

To play the game:
  • You will need two people to play. Share our link with a friend to get started.
  • Give your stand in a catchy name.
  • Wait for your friend to arrive or send them a quick message to let them know you are waiting for them.
  • You will be presented with the rock, paper, scissors choices to attack/defend against your opponent.
  • Feel free to antagonize while you wait for their response, once they do you will know if you have won the round or not.
  • Keep playing until your heart's desire and we will present you with a tally of your wins/losses.
  • If you opponent leaves don't worry you can go ahead and wait for another friend to show up or play against the computer.
